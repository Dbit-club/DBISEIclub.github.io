Founder's view

Don Bosco ISE Innovation Lab has been established to provide a platform and mechanism for the internal & External stakeholders to interact with each other and thus to foster needs/opportunities based on innovation.
The importance of education and innovation is undisputed in helping a nation become a global leader and in maintaining its leadership. Taking into account the diverse needs of people from across India, regional growth becomes the foundation for national development.
The Don Bosco ISE Innovation Lab nurtures innovation and encourages entrepreneurial talents among students, faculty, and people of the region. The Innovation Lab seeks to build an innovation network with multiple stakeholders like Industry, other institutions, R&D Labs, and others.
This Lab will act as the focal point and will be able to leverage:

  • The technology R&D and problem solving strengths of the DBISEIL
  • The entrepreneurial spirit of the students and faculty
  • Collaboration with local industry, NGOs, and others
  • The teaching and training capabilities of the DBISEIL
  • Government policy initiatives more efficiently

Innovation club

Innovation Lab - 2020 is initiated by the Department of Information Science & Engineering, under the leadership of Mrs. Gowramma G S, Head of the Department, ISE, DBIT.
The main motive of the lab is to encourage students to gain knowledge in various technological fields, acquire the required skillsets to face the competitive world, and improve themselves to become industry-ready.
The introductory session for the Lab was held on the 8th of November, 2020. The session was hosted and addressed by Mrs.Gowramma G S and Mr.Swaraj Kumar, founder of ‘School of IoT’, Student, DBIT.

Topics discussed in the Introductory session are as follows:

  • Introduction to the Innovation Lab and its main Objective.
  • An Approach towards the Lab and its Events.
  • Encouragement to the students to be a part of the Initiative.
  • A Glimpse of the conduction of Several Activities.
  • Announcement of the Courses, Teams, and Team Leaders.

The introductory session brought all the students together to know the reason for the initiation of the Lab and the conduction of various activities. It was a very interactive session in which the students came up with their ideas and suggestions in addition to the proposed ones. Few students initiated themselves to serve as team leaders to different groups. The students straightforwardly raised their comments on the topics which were not understandable by them and cleared their ambiguity with the host.

Blockchain technology is a form of digitalized, de-centralized public record of all cryptocurrency transactions.
Blockchain was designed to record, not just financial-related transactions, but virtually everything of value.
I'll say it's gold for nerds . It is fascinating how blockchain technology works. That's what keep me up to know more about it.
The need for decentralization is the key motivation behind the blockchain technology, and decentralization is achieved by distributing the computation tasks to all the nodes of the blockchain network.
Why is Blockchain Popular?

  • Blockchain can be used for secure transaction (end to end) with out any third party app
  • Blockchain is a digital ledger that is gaining a lot of attention and traction.
  • Blockchain can be used to store the data (As a database which is secured)

Most people assume Blockchain and Bitcoin can be used interchangeably, but in reality, that’s not the case. Blockchain is the technology capable of supporting various applications related to multiple industries like finance, supply chain, manufacturing, etc., but Bitcoin is a currency that relies on Blockchain technology to be secure.
Purpose

  • To analyse blockchain use cases in both financial and non-financial fields.
  • To generate new ideas and to be familiar with the current affairs related to blockchain
Facilities
  • Resources to enhance knowledge on Blockchain Technology.
  • Programming Languages and software : Python, Solidity, Java etc.
  • Learn more about Ethereum and daApps
